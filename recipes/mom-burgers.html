Mama's Homemade Burgers

My mama's homemade burgers are just the pest thing. Some of my fondest childhood movies have the smell of fries and beef patties cooking up. And these burgers were no joke, she spent a lot of time and effort making the absolute best sandwhiches possbile, "With all the fixin's" she'd say. This recipe is a tribute to here fantastic home cooking. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Big Mac®
  • 1 *Medium Fry, optional
  • 1 *Medium Coca-Cola or other soft drink, optional
* You can upgrade to a large, if you're feeling up to it

Instructions

  1. Drive to your local McDonalds
  2. Park the car and *enter the store
  3. Order the above ingredients
  4. Wait for your order to be completed, and now you enjoy my Mam's homemade bugers
* You can also order throught the drive-thru, but inside is a more complete experience
